Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Tip Calculator app. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your data and ensure your privacy while using our app. By using the Tip Calculator, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal or non-personal data. All calculations and operations occur locally on your device, ensuring that no information leaves your device.

3. Data Usage

The app processes the data you enter solely for the purpose of calculating tips. This information is used locally and is not transmitted to any external servers or third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

The Tip Calculator app does not integrate with any third-party services, ensuring that your data remains entirely private.

5. Security

We prioritize your privacy by designing the app to function without the need for any internet connection or external data sharing. This approach ensures maximum security for your information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted here with the updated date.
